## Dataset
I used [2025 Stack Overflow Annual Developer Survey Results](https://survey.stackoverflow.co/). Stack Overflow is a platform for developers to ask questions and share knowledge. Its annual survey is designed to capture the developer experience, and it is the longest-running and most comprehensive study of its kind. The 2025 survey contained questions on learning and career, tech and tech culture, community, and AI. It received 49,000+ responses from developers across 177 countries.

## Purpose
I chose this dataset because burnout is a growing concern in the tech industry, often going undetected until it affects performance and retention. Analyzing developer survey data can help organizations identify which factors, such as careers, experiences, and tools are most associated with burnout risk.
